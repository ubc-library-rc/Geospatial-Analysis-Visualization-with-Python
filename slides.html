Todays Objective

This dataset may be difficult or triggering for some to work with as it focuses on a very painful subject. We are going to examine a dataset of Police Killings in Canada from 2000-2017. The data was obtained from Pivot legal society and initally collected by the CBC. We're going to look for paterns and disparities in Police Killings by race, gender, and other factors. The end goal of this workshop is to show that programming and data analysis languages can be powerful tools for promoting soicail and envrionmental justice issues.
